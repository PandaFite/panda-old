Moderator Application

If you would like to apply for moderator in this channel you must have over 40 hours in the stream and be active in chat. You may check your time in the stream by doing !time. Send a message with the subject "Moderator Application" to tell me why you want to be a channel moderator and your time zone. Then I will reply telling you if you were accepted within 7 days. Asking about your moderator application in stream chat will disqualify you (and probably a time-out, too!)
